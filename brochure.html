What we do

One company.
Eight disciplines.

PRML RECORDS is a vertically integrated creative-technology company. Music to manufacturing, code to live events. Everything in-house, everything connected.

Make

We design and produce.

Music production, beat licensing, mixing and mastering. Branded merchandise via 3D printing, laser cutting, and UV printing. Custom signs, NFC business cards, keychains, light boxes, t-shirts, banners. Full creative direction from sketch to final product.

Manufacturing capacity scaling from 1 printer to 30 across the year. Bulk turnaround as fast as 1 business day at full capacity.

Ship

We deliver and distribute.

Web development, brand identity, e-commerce, NFC technology integration. Direct-to-consumer through prmlrecords.com, partner streaming platforms for music, and physical fulfillment from our Atlanta facility.

Real-time order tracking, automated invoicing, customer support that actually answers. We do this for ourselves and we do it for clients.

Perform

We activate in person.

Beltline pop-up series starting May 2026 (weekly, Ponce City Market and Kroger on Ponce). Live performance through SEAUX9 and our roster. Festival activations - Juneteenth Atlanta, Tassili's Natural Mystic Festival, FIFA 2026 county arts programming.

Booth space, branded swag distribution, sponsor visibility, live music, content capture - all from one team.

Connect

We bridge people.

Bilingual, multicultural, founder-led. We work with artists ready to build long-term, businesses that need creative-tech work done right, and partners aligned with our values.
