Who We Are
Built In
West End.

PRML Records is a Black, Latino, Indigenous, and Haitian owned creative studio rooted in West End Atlanta. We design, manufacture, and market physical and digital media — connecting art fanatics, collectors, and enthusiasts directly to the artists and work that move them. We serve creatives, consumers, and businesses. Our roots are local. Our reach is global.
