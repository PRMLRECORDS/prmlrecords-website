Core Competencies

PRML Records LLC is a Black-owned creative technology company headquartered in the West End neighborhood of Atlanta, Georgia. We deliver integrated solutions across music production, media, technology, and community engagement — operating at the intersection of culture and innovation to serve independent artists, small businesses, nonprofits, and government entities.

Section 03
Differentiators

What sets PRML Records apart from competing vendors and service providers in our market categories.

01
Vertically Integrated Creative Stack
Audio, video, photography, print, web, and consulting under one roof — eliminating the coordination friction of managing multiple vendors for creative projects.
02
Community-Rooted, Culture-Forward
Embedded in West End Atlanta — a historically significant Black creative district. Our work centers community impact, authentic storytelling, and economic empowerment alongside commercial deliverables.
03
Technology-Enabled Delivery
Custom-built backend systems using Google Workspace, automation pipelines, and proprietary web infrastructure allow us to deliver faster, more consistently, and at competitive price points.
04
Emerging MWBE & SBA-Ready
Actively pursuing City of Atlanta MWBE, SAM.gov, HUBZone, and SBA 8(a) certifications — positioned to fulfill small business set-aside contract requirements for government and institutional buyers.
05
Founder-Led Accountability
Every engagement is personally overseen by the founder. No junior handoffs, no outsourced quality gaps — clients receive direct access to senior creative and strategic expertise from day one.
06
Agile & Responsive
As a growing small business we move faster than large agencies. Projects that would take weeks at a traditional firm are delivered in days, without sacrificing professional-grade quality.
