🌳 Decision Trees — What to Do When
You have 10 free hours this week. What do you focus on?
If you need revenue NOW: Reach out to 10 local businesses about NFC signs + follow up on any open quotes.
If you want bookings: Post a performance clip on IG, update your EPK, and message 5 Atlanta venues directly.
If you want passive income: Record one beat, upload it to a lease platform (BeatStars/AirBit), and create the digital download section.
If you're building for the future: Write one blog post, create a workshop outline, and apply for the MWBE certification.
A client wants a quote. What do you charge?
Custom product: Material cost × 3 + $20 setup + $6 processing. Never below your time × $25/hr.
Service: Listed rate on site. If they push back, hold your price — discount signals low confidence.
First 9 customers: Waive the setup fee only. Everything else is full price.
Consulting: $150/hr or package. Consultation counts as deposit if they move forward.
You want to market PRML Records. Where do you spend your time?
Instagram: Best ROI for visual products (NFC signs, light boxes, keychains) and SEAUX9 performance clips. Post 3×/week minimum.
Google Business: Critical for local service discovery. Keep it updated. Ask every happy customer to leave a review.
LinkedIn: For B2B — consultants, real estate agents, dealerships. Best channel for retainer clients.
TikTok/Reels: Behind-the-scenes of 3D printing, laser work, and recording sessions. These go viral fast in maker communities.
