What it is

One card.
Infinite connections.

The PRML TAP CARD is a premium NFC-enabled business card — the same size and feel as a credit card. Tap it to any modern smartphone and your full digital profile opens instantly in their browser: your name, phone, email, website, socials, portfolio, booking link — everything you want to share.

No app on their end. No subscription on your end. No reprinting when things change. We program the chip, ship it to you, and you tap your way through life.

01
Tap
Hold your PRML TAP CARD near any smartphone. No apps, no Bluetooth, no friction. Works on all iPhones (XS+) and all modern Android.
02
Connect
Their phone opens your digital profile instantly — contact info, socials, portfolio, booking link, or anything you choose.
03
Done
They save your info. You made the connection. No paper. No fumbling. No forgotten cards.
