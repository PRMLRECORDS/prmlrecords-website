About PRML RECORDS

PRML RECORDS LLC (pronounced "Primal") is a Black-owned creative design, marketing, and manufacturing company based in Atlanta, Georgia. Founded in 2019, PRML RECORDS has evolved from a record label into a full-service creative company serving artists, businesses, and communities.

Operations include music production, branded NFC products, web development, photography, film production, and consulting services — all built on a foundation of Freedom, Justice, and Equality.
