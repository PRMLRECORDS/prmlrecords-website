Got questions?
We've got answers.
Does the person I tap need an app?
No. When you tap your PRML TAP CARD or TAP KEY to their phone, their browser opens your profile page automatically. It works on all iPhones (XS and newer) and all modern Android phones. No downloads, no friction.
Is there a monthly fee or subscription?
No. You pay once and the card is yours forever. No subscriptions, no renewals, no platform fees. That's the fundamental difference between PRML TAP and services like Popl or Dot.
Can I update my profile after I receive the card?
Yes. Your digital profile can be updated anytime — new phone number, new social handle, new booking link — without reprinting the card. The NFC chip links to your profile, not to fixed data.
What's the difference between the TAP CARD and TAP KEY?
The PRML TAP CARD is a standard card format — same size as a credit card, slim and clean. The PRML TAP KEY attaches to your keychain. Same NFC technology, same profile — just a different form factor for people who always have their keys but not always their wallet.
What does "setup fee waived" mean for first 9 customers?
Setup includes loading your digital profile, programming the NFC chip, and configuring your landing page. For the first 9 customers, we waive that fee entirely — you only pay the product price plus the $6 processing fee.
How long does shipping take?
Single cards and keys ship in 3–5 business days from Atlanta, GA. 10-packs ship in 5–7 business days. Need it faster? Contact us at 770-686-7726 and we'll do what we can.
