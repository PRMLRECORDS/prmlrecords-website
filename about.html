The Company

We make. We ship. We perform.

PRML RECORDS is a Black, Latino, Indigenous, and Haitian owned creative studio rooted in West End Atlanta. We design, manufacture, and market physical and digital media - connecting art fanatics, collectors, and enthusiasts directly to the artists and work that move them.

We serve creatives, consumers, and businesses. Our roots are local. Our reach is global. The work spans music production, NFC products, custom signs, web development, photography, film, and manufacturing - all in-house, all under one roof.

We believe in long-game relationships, honest pricing, and showing up for our community. No shortcuts, no extractive tactics. Just real work for real people.
